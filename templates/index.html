Organ and Tissue Donation

Organ donation involves transferring organs from a living or deceased person to someone in need.

What is Organ and Tissue Donation?

Organ donation is a pledge to donate organs post-death to save or improve lives.

What is Transplantation?

Transplantation is the transfer of cells, tissues, or organs to restore vital functions when alternatives aren't available.

Who Can Donate?

Anyone 18+ who voluntarily agrees can donate organs/tissues for transplantation purposes.
